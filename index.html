What it is

QR Photo Transfer is a desktop app that moves photos and images between your phone and computer over your local network.

You scan a QR code on your phone, pick or take images, and they appear in a folder on your PC (or the other way around)—no cloud uploads, no extra apps to re-download from, no manual saving from chat or Drive.

Why QR Photo Transfer

  • No cloud uploads — Images go device-to-device; no Google Drive, Dropbox, or email.
  • No chat workarounds — No need to send to WhatsApp, Discord, or Telegram and re-download.
  • Straight to a folder — Received images save directly into a folder you choose on your computer.
  • Simple flow — Scan QR → choose photos → they appear on your PC (or phone).
  • Your network only — Transfer over your Wi‑Fi; you stay in control of your photos.

Features

Transfer & sessions

Send images either way: phone to PC or PC to phone. Choose direction, then scan the QR or use the link. The desktop shows a QR code (and soon a shareable URL) so you can open the session on your phone—no app store install. Shareable link for each session is in development.

On your computer & phone

  • Computer: Choose where received files go (e.g. Pictures); default is a “QR Photo Transfer” folder. Light, dark, or system theme; auto-updates with one-click restart and install.
  • Phone (PWA): Save to Photos/Gallery or download; works in the browser, no app store—add to home screen for an app-like experience.

Premium

Unlimited transfers (free tier: 5 per direction per day). Create temporary QR codes with an expiry you set (e.g. 5–120 minutes)—good for one-off shares or kiosks; the code stops working after the chosen time. Manage your subscription (upgrade, change plan, or cancel) via Stripe’s billing portal.

Security & privacy

Images flow device-to-device; we don’t store them on our servers. Sign in (e.g. Google) so only you use your sessions. Only valid session IDs are accepted; expired or invalid links/QRs are rejected.

FAQ

What is QR Photo Transfer?
It’s a desktop app (plus a web app on your phone) that moves images between your phone and computer. You connect by scanning a QR code (or soon, opening a link), then send photos either way—no need to upload to Google Drive, WhatsApp, or Discord and re-download.
Do I need to install something on my phone?
No native app. You open a link in your browser (the same link encoded in the QR). You can add that page to your home screen so it behaves like an app.
Where do the images go on my computer?
They’re saved in a folder you choose. By default it’s a folder like “QR Photo Transfer” in your Pictures directory; you can change this in the desktop app settings.
Can I send from computer to phone too?
Yes. You pick “PC → Phone,” then scan the QR (or open the link) on your phone. Images you select on the desktop are sent to your phone, where you can save to Photos/Gallery or download.
What are “temporary” QR codes?
Premium feature. You set an expiry (e.g. 10 or 60 minutes). After that, that QR/link stops working. Good for one-time shares or when you don’t want a long-lived session.
Will there be a way to transfer without scanning the QR?
Yes. We’re adding shareable links for each session. You’ll be able to open the same session in your phone browser via a URL, so you can transfer without scanning—e.g. from another device or when you can’t use the camera. Currently in development.
Is there a limit on how many images I can transfer?
Free accounts have a limit (e.g. 5 transfers per direction per day). Premium has unlimited transfers. “Transfer” here means one send operation (which can include multiple images in that batch).
Are my photos uploaded to your servers?
Images pass through our backend to connect your phone and computer but are not stored. We don’t keep copies on our servers.
What do I need to run the desktop app?
Windows, macOS, or Linux. Download the installer from the website (or GitHub Releases), install, then sign in and follow the in-app steps.
How do I get Premium?
In the app, open Profile and use “Upgrade to Premium.” You’ll complete payment in your browser; after that, Premium features (unlimited transfers, temporary QR codes) are available. If the app still shows Free, use “Refresh plan status” or restart the app.
